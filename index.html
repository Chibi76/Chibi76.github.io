Recipe #1

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients ------------- Quantity
Elbow Macaroni 16 oz.
Butter 1/4 Cup
All-Purpose Flour 1/4 Cup
Salt 1/2 Teaspoon
Black Pepper 1 Dash
Milk (Warmed) 2 Cups
Cheddar Cheese 8 oz.

Step 1: Cook macaroni according to package directions.

Step 2: In medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; stir in flour, and cook for 3-5 minutes stirring constantly to form a roux; add salt and pepper; slowly add milk, stirring well after each addition.

Step 3: Cook and stir until bubbly.

Step 4: Stir in cheese a small amount at a time until fully melted.

Step 5: Drain macaroni; add to cheese sauce; stir to coat.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 5min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients ------- Quantity
Eggs (beaten) 2 Large Eggs
Sugar 3 Tablespoons
Low-Fat Milk 2 1/3 Cups
Vanilla Extract 1 Teaspoon
Ground Nutmeg 1 Dash

Step 1: Blend together eggs, sugar, milk, vanilla and nutmeg. Serve chilled.

Editor's Note: This recipe contains raw egg. We recommend that pregnant women, young children, the elderly, and the infirm do not consume raw egg.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 10min
  • Yeild: 4 dozen
Ingredients -------------- Quantity
All-Purpose Flour 2 Â¾ Cups
Baking Soda 1 Teaspoon
Baking Powder 1/2 Teaspoon
Softened Butter 1 Cup
White Sugar 1 1/2 Cups
Egg 1 Large Egg
Vanilla Extract 1 Teaspoon
Buttermilk 3 Tablespoons
Colored Sugar Preference

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Step 2: In a small bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.

Step 3: In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg and vanilla. Gradually blend in dry ingredients. Add enough of the buttermilk to moisten the dough and make it soft, not wet.

Step 4: Roll rounded teaspoons of dough into balls and place on a ungreased cookie sheet. With a brush or fingers, moisten the top of each cookie with the remaining buttermilk and slightly flatten the top of each cookie. Sprinkle with raw sugar or colored sprinkles.

Step 5: Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until slightly golden. Let stand for 2 minutes before removing to cool on a rack.
